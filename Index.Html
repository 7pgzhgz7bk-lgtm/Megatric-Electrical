Our Story

BUILT ON
HONESTY &
HARD WORK

Megatric Electrical CC was founded with a simple mission: provide dependable electrical services with honesty, quality workmanship, and rapid response times. As a growing company, every client matters, and every project is completed with care and professionalism.

We built Megatric because we saw a gap — too many clients waiting days for repairs, receiving unclear quotes, or dealing with work that simply wasn't done right. We set out to change that, one job at a time.

Based in Namibia, we serve residential homes, commercial properties, and industrial sites with the same level of dedication. We're growing fast because our clients trust us — and we work hard to earn that trust every single day.
